Key Personnel

Ironward · Hearthsworn · Tidecrest

Compiled by the Avandrian Academy Intelligence Office for distribution to senior expeditioners. Profiles represent our best current assessment. All information subject to revision.

Faction One
Ironward Logistics
"Merit earns the march."
⚙

Ironward is the largest and best-resourced of the three companies. It recruits on ability alone, no noble blood, no questions about the past, no loyalty owed to anyone who didn't earn it. Its camps are orderly, its logistics are precise, and its leadership is the most intellectually formidable on the continent. Ironward does not ask you to believe in anything. It asks you to perform.

C
Calder Voss
Director of Operations · Ironward Logistics
Founder Male Human, Age 39
Assessment
The most dangerous mind on the continent. Calder Voss built Ironward from nothing on a single conviction, that talent is the only currency that matters, and has never once wavered from it. His logistical networks keep camps alive in conditions that should be fatal. His intelligence operations know things before they happen. He is also a man who has decided, somewhere along the way, that the ends justify the means more often than most people are comfortable with. He will not announce this. He will simply proceed.
Known Traits
Unshakeable composure under pressure. Genuinely respects competence wherever he finds it, including in enemies. Uncomfortable with sentiment, not because he lacks it, but because he considers it a liability. Has advisors he trusts and gives them genuine room to operate. Slow to anger. When he is finally angry, it is architectural: deliberate, structural, designed to last.
A
Aldrin Dunmore
Field Commander · Ironward Logistics
Commander Female Human, Age 35
Assessment
One-eyed, unhurried, and possessed of the specific calm that belongs only to people who have already survived everything that should have killed them. Aldrin Dunmore has been with Calder long enough to push back on him, and loyal enough that she almost never does. Even if their job is logistics, Aldrin is the reason why Ironward will also be able to pull their weight in a battle against the Collector.
Known Traits
Blunt. Respectful of demonstrated courage regardless of rank. Her relationship with Calder is the oldest in Ironward; she is one of the few people who knows the difference between who he is and who he presents as, and has made her peace with both.
D
Davan Wrek
Personal Guard · Ironward Logistics
Bodyguard Male Dwarf, Age 28
Assessment
Enormous. Quiet. Terrifying in the specific way of things that do not feel the need to announce themselves. Davan Wrek is Calder Voss's personal bodyguard, a position he has held since before Ironward existed, and his loyalty is not to the company, it is to the man. This distinction matters. He will follow orders from Ironward's chain of command when those orders align with Calder's wellbeing. When they conflict, the chain of command does not win.
Known Traits
Does not waste words. Has a dry, very occasional humor that surprises people who assumed he had none. Observes everything. Forgets nothing. Is one of perhaps three people on the continent Calder Voss visibly relaxes around. If Davan is concerned, something is wrong.
G
Gia Hollow
Senior Strategist · Ironward Logistics
Strategist Female Elf, Age 21
Assessment
The one person in Ironward that Calder Voss listens to without reservation. Brilliant in a way that is somehow also irritating, she reaches correct conclusions through routes nobody else sees, then presents them with the casual energy of someone describing the weather. She has no patience for people she doesn't respect and does not pretend otherwise, which has made her universally regarded and selectively liked. She is also, by most indications, unwell. She burns at an intensity her body was not designed to sustain.
Known Traits
Irreverent. Acutely perceptive. Finds most social protocol tedious and skips it when she can get away with it. Her relationship with Calder is the closest thing she has to a genuine peer dynamic, she is not afraid of him, which he finds refreshing and occasionally alarming.
Faction Two
Hearthsworn Company
"We go together or not at all."
🜂

The smallest of the three companies and the one people seem to believe in most. Hearthsworn does not run on systems or bloodlines, it runs on something harder to quantify: the sense that these people would genuinely die for one another, and mean it. It was built by three childhood friends who came up from nothing and have never once stopped having each other's backs. It is the faction for people who need the cause to matter as much as the outcome.

L
Loric Ashvane
Commander and Co-founder · Hearthsworn Company
Co-founder Male Elf, age 27
Assessment
Warm in the specific way that is indistinguishable from dangerous if you mistake warmth for weakness. Loric Ashvane has never asked anyone to follow him. They follow because he makes them believe that what they are doing together is worth the cost of doing it. He is principled to a degree that his allies find admirable and his enemies find exploitable, and he knows both are true and has decided it does not matter. His dream is not profit. He wants to actually help the people they find on this continent, and in the absence of people, he has expanded the definition to include the world itself.
Known Traits
Slow to judge, slow to condemn, impossible to corrupt through direct means. Can be moved by appeals to fairness that a more cynical leader would dismiss. Leans heavily on Gareth and Reni more than he admits. The gap between his instincts and the right answer is small.
G
Gareth Yu
Field Captain and Co-founder · Hearthsworn Company
Co-founder Male Human, age 27
Assessment
Towering, deliberate, and in possession of a personal code of conduct that he has never once compromised in his life. Other warriors want Gareth's respect the way merchants want gold, it means something, because he does not give it carelessly. He is the moral anchor Hearthsworn moves around without fully acknowledging it does so. When he speaks in a meeting, the room listens. Not because of his rank. Because he is almost always right, and everyone in the room knows the last time he was wrong they wished they had listened.
Known Traits
A particular softness for people who have been betrayed by someone they trusted, he recognizes it and takes it seriously. Has a complicated relationship with patience: infinite toward people he is teaching, very limited toward people wasting his time.
R
Reni Calloway
Field Captain and Co-Founder · Hearthsworn Company
Co-founder Female Gnome, age 26
Assessment
Loud, impulsive, hilarious, and absolutely ferocious when it counts. Reni Calloway is the youngest of the founding trio and the one most likely to adopt a stranger as a lifelong companion within an hour of meeting them. She is also the one most likely to hold a grudge on behalf of those companions for decades after they've moved on. Her protectiveness over Loric and Gareth is the most uncomplicated thing about her. Despite her stature, she somehow feels like the biggest presence in a room.
Known Traits
Picks people up instantly and completely. Zero patience for condescension, social formality, or being underestimated because of how she presents. Has a temper that runs hot and cools quickly. Is genuinely funnier than she has any right to be given how much of the time she is threatening to hit someone.
Faction Three
Tidecrest Shipping
"Family holds the shore."
⚓

The oldest money of the three. Tidecrest was founded by the Solane family and has been a Solane company ever since, that is not nepotism, that is simply how they understand the world. Blood is the only thing you can truly trust, and everyone who earns a place under Tidecrest's banner becomes, in some operational sense, family. They are diplomatically sophisticated, strategically patient, and possessed of a network of merchants that none can match. They also protect their own with a ferocity that surprises people who mistook their civility for softness.

E
Edric Solane
Founding Patriarch · Tidecrest Shipping
Founder Male Human, age 62
Assessment
A genuine legend, which is a description he finds faintly embarrassing and entirely accurate. Edric Solane was on the first ship that ever made the crossing to Dorenshire. He planted Tidecrest's flag here before there was a Tidecrest worth speaking of. He is semi-retired in the operational sense and completely irreplaceable in every other sense, his name opens doors, smooths disputes, and carries a weight of credibility that cannot be manufactured. Even Calder Voss speaks of him with something approaching genuine respect.
Known Traits
Patient in the way of people who have seen enough to know that most urgency is manufactured. Deeply, quietly loving toward his children in a manner he expresses through action rather than words.
A
Eren Solane
In-Field Commander · Tidecrest Shipping
In-Field Commander Female Human, age 27
Assessment
Beloved by her people in a way that borders on devotion, they would do anything for her, and she knows it, and mostly uses that power well. She is the best battlefield commander on the continent and knows that too. The problem is the gap between her judgment and her impulses, which narrows under pressure in exactly the wrong direction. But in spite of it all, she's probably the most charismatic leader one could ask for, even if she goes off plan, orders her men into a burning building, they'll all be there, cause she'll be the first one through the door.
Known Traits
Magnetic. Generous with credit when things go well. His friendship with Zolan Ife is the most honest relationship she has — Zolan is the one person who tells her things he doesn't want to hear, but, he's not on this expedition.
Q
Quinn Solane
Operational Commander · Tidecrest Company
Operational Commander Male Human, age 25
Assessment
Younger brother of Eren. More patient. A better listener. Currently operating in his sister's considerable shadow and doing nothing to escape it, not out of deference, but because he is paying close attention to everything happening around him and shadow is a useful place to do that from. He is not in the spotlight, but he is the one in the family paying the most attention to how all the pieces fit together.
Known Traits
Underestimated constantly. Finds it useful rather than frustrating. Asks questions that seem simple and are not. His relationship with Mira is the most functional and most complicated dynamic in the faction: total trust, complete honesty, occasionally difficult conclusions.
M
Mira Solane
Intelligence · Tidecrest Company
Intelligence Female Human, age 25
Assessment
Younger than Quinn by 7 minutes. If Quinn's in the shadows, she's practicially invisible, she's been building a personal network across all three factions since before the expedition, collecting information and favors with the systematic patience of someone playing a longer game than anyone around her has identified. She is publicly the faction's diplomat. Privately she is Tidecrest's most dangerous asset, and she has done things in service of her family that Edric and Eren never even knew about.
Known Traits
Reads rooms faster than most people read sentences. Skilled with a blade and more skilled in a negotiation. Has genuine warmth for people she respects, which she extends carefully and withdraws completely.
A
Aldous Fenn
Senior Retainer · Tidecrest Company
Body Guard Male Dwarf, age 74
Assessment
The oldest man in Tidecrest and the one who has been with the Solane family the longest, since before Edric was who Edric became. His devotion to the family has outlasted the specific people it started with. He served Edric completely, and when the time comes, Eren, Quinn and Mira, not because the succession is legally binding but because the Solanes are what he has organized his life around, and he is not a man who reorganizes.
Known Traits
Weathered past the point of impressiveness into something quieter and more final. Has seen every version of this family and carries that history as institutional memory no document could replicate. Speaks rarely in group settings. When he does speak, the Solanes listen, all of them, including Eren, which is telling. His loyalty is not complicated. It is the least complicated thing in Tidecrest, which makes it the most stable.

Profiles compiled from field observation, Academy records, and direct interview where possible.
